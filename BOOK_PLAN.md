# Working plan for the MCLF book

This document records the current working plan for the book.
It is provisional and will be updated as the project develops.


## Guiding idea

The book develops the theory of models of curves and valuations, connects it with Berkovich and Mac Lane techniques, and applies these tools to semistable reduction and \(p\)-cyclic covers.
